Welcome To The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda

The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda (MSU), state university, established in 1949, located in Vadodara, Gujarat, India. It is a residential university and its origin can be traced back to the School of Agriculture, established in 1890. MSU is one of the premier institutions of higher learning in Western India, offering world-class courses in Engineering, Technology, Science, Commerce, Fine Arts, and Humanities.
